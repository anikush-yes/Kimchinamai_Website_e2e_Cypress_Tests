Functionality of the website https://kimchinamai.lt


1. Navigation Menu: 

Provides access to different sections like "Pagrindinis" (Home), "Parduotuvė" (Store) - contains dropdown menu with product selection, "Tinklaraštis" (Blog), and "Kontaktai" (Contact).

2. Product Categories: 

Under "Parduotuvė", products are organized into categories such as "Kimchi", "Rinkiniai" (Sets), "Probiotinės sultys" (Probiotic Juices), "Fermentuoti padažai" (Fermented Sauces), and "Kita" (Other).

3. Product Listings: 

Each category showcases products with details like name, price, description, and "Į krepšelį"(Add to Cart) button, "Prekių pristatymas" (Delivery)

4. Shopping Cart: 

Accessible via the "Krepšelis" (Cart) icon, it displays selected products, quantities, and total price, with options to update quantities or remove items.

5. User Account: 

Users can log in or register through the "Prisijungti" (Log In) option to manage personal information and view order history.

6. Search Function: 

A "Prekių paieška" (Product Search) feature allows users to find specific products by entering keywords.

7. Blog: 

The "Tinklaraštis" (Blog) section offers articles related to kimchi, its benefits, and usage tips.

8. Contact Information: 

The "Kontaktai" (Contact) page provides contact details and a form for customer inquiries.

9. Footer Links: 

Includes quick links to "Privatumo politika" (Privacy Policy), "Sąlygos ir taisyklės" (Terms and Conditions), "Prekių pristatymas" (Product Delivery), and "DUK" (FAQ).
